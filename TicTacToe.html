A Classic Game of Tic-Tac-Toe !

Player 1 = 'X'
player 2 = 'O'

Welcome!

Here we have the classic game of Tic Tac Toe! Enter you and a friends name to get started.

Rules:
-Player 1 and Player 2 will both take turns placing an "X" or an "O" in each square.
- The Player that has 3 "X's" or "O's" in a row wins the game!
Enjoy!
